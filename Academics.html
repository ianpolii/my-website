Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten Program at TIS nurtures the early development of young children in a loving, Christ-centered environment. We focus on fostering curiosity, creativity, and social skills through age-appropriate activities that introduce foundational concepts of faith and learning. Guided by caring teachers, toddlers begin their educational journey with a strong spiritual and academic foundation.

Primary

At TIS, our Primary Program builds a strong academic and spiritual foundation for young learners. We integrate a Christ-centered curriculum with core subjects, encouraging students to explore their God-given talents and develop a love for learning. Through engaging lessons, interactive activities, and a supportive community, children grow academically, spiritually, and socially, preparing them for the next stage of their educational journey.

Junior Highschool

Our Junior High Program at TIS guides students through a critical time of growth and discovery. We provide a rigorous academic curriculum intertwined with Christian values, helping students strengthen their faith while developing essential skills for high school and beyond. In a nurturing environment, students are encouraged to think critically, act responsibly, and deepen their relationship with Christ as they prepare for the future.

Senior Highschool

The Senior High School Program at TIS equips students for college, careers, and a life of purpose rooted in Christian faith. We offer challenging academic curriculums such as GAC, combined with opportunities for spiritual growth, leadership development, and community service. Our goal is to prepare students to excel in their chosen paths while staying grounded in their relationship with Christ. Through personalized guidance and a supportive environment, we help students confidently step into adulthood, ready to make a positive impact in the world.
