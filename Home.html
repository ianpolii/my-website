Our History

The origin of the present day TIS started in 2002 when school founders formed a small preschool "Little Morning Star" in Kelapa Gading. The school educational system was based on Christian principles and bible character based. In 2007, to meet a demand for the educational needs of the primary level, Teach Indonesia School was established, by inviting new shareholders to strengthen the school basis. In Bahasa Indonesia, the school name is also known as sekolah Tunas Indonesia Sejati. Until today, the highest level available is up to year 9 (Junior High).

The foundation of Teach Indonesia School educational system continues to be based on the Christian principles and bible character. The students, from the baby class until the highest education level, are taught to find and understand their life purpose and embedded the honorable characters into their daily life. The right learning concept and knowledge of why they have to study will assist the students to achieve academic excellence.

In TIS, the academic program has been designed to allow students to build their characters and to develop competence in knowledge and life skills. We tailor our education program by combining best Christian curriculums to ensure that our school's academic standard is excellence. Our enrichment program will also equip our students with essential life skills and eye-open them to value life-long learning.
